About

SnakeWifAi is a Play2Earn crypto game on the TON Blockchain. Dive into the neon retro world of SnakeWifAi and earn crypto rewards!

How It Works

We make learning about AI as fun as playing a game. You’ll guide an AI through the process of mastering Snake, tweaking its “brain” and strategy as it learns to navigate the grid, gobble up food, and avoid crashing into walls (or itself!). The better you train your AI, the higher your score—and your bragging rights—will soar.

>

Compete & Win

Once your AI is up and running, put it to the test! Compete against other trainers to see whose AI snake reigns supreme. Our dynamic leaderboard showcases the best of the best, offering a platform for friendly competition and inspiration. Whether you’re a tech enthusiast, a gaming pro, or just curious about AI, there’s a spot on the leaderboard with your name on it.
